Houdini

Nuke

Maya

In Maya, I create complex shapes using multiple modeling techniques such as polygon and NURBS modeling. I’m also experienced in setting up lighting and rendering with Arnold, including working with AOVs to provide greater control and flexibility in compositing.

3D-Scanning

Video Editing & Color Grading
